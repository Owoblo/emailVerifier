=== TEST ALL FORMATS ===

Copy any of these examples into the platform:

1. EXCEL/TABLE FORMAT (pipe-separated):
John Doe | google.com
Jane Smith | microsoft.com
Elon Musk | tesla.com

2. CSV FORMAT:
John, Doe, google.com
Jane, Smith, microsoft.com
Elon, Musk, tesla.com

3. WITH URLs:
John Doe https://google.com
Jane Smith www.microsoft.com
Elon Musk http://tesla.com

4. MESSY TEXT (like from documents):
John Doe at Google (google.com)
Jane Smith from Microsoft - microsoft.com
Contact: Elon Musk, Tesla Inc. www.tesla.com

5. TAB-SEPARATED (copy from Excel):
John	Doe	google.com
Jane	Smith	microsoft.com
Elon	Musk	tesla.com

6. JUST NAMES AND DOMAINS:
John Doe google.com
Jane Smith microsoft.com
Elon Musk tesla.com

7. REVERSE FORMAT (Last, First):
DOE, John google.com
SMITH, Jane microsoft.com
MUSK, Elon tesla.com

8. MIXED CHAOS (it'll figure it out):
John Doe | Company: Google | Website: https://google.com
Jane Smith, works at Microsoft (www.microsoft.com)
Elon Musk - Tesla Inc - tesla.com

=== THE PLATFORM HANDLES ALL OF THESE! ===

Just paste whatever you have. It extracts:
- First Name
- Last Name
- Domain (from URLs, plain domains, anywhere in the text)

Then generates 8 email variations and verifies them all.
